# Publication Policy

The wiki is intended to be accessible from outside the development environment.
Treat it as public by default unless repository access controls say otherwise.

The public wiki is still proprietary work by Francisco Amadeo. Public access does
not grant a license to copy, reuse, redistribute, commercialize, train on, or
implement the research, hypotheses, experiment designs, strategy logic, or
derived conclusions.

## Ownership

- Owner: Francisco Amadeo.
- Rights: All rights reserved unless Francisco Amadeo grants explicit written
  permission.
- Scope: hypotheses, experiment designs, source summaries, wiki text,
  methodology, strategy logic, analysis, and derived conclusions.
- Access model: public documentation access, not open-source or open-data
  licensing.

## Publish

- Experiment IDs, titles, hypotheses, decisions, and high-level methods.
- Summary metrics, rejection reasons, drawdowns, costs, and robustness notes.
- Non-sensitive universe descriptions and root symbols.
- Methodological caveats, failed tests, and open questions.

## Do Not Publish

- API keys, `.env` contents, broker credentials, or account identifiers.
- Raw Databento files, parquet datasets, MLflow databases, or local caches.
- Absolute local filesystem paths except when intentionally documenting a local
  setup guide.
- Vendor-licensed bulk data or reconstructed datasets.
- Any statement that a strategy has edge unless it has passed the promotion
  gates in [promotion-gates.md](promotion-gates.md).

## Generated Pages

Experiment pages under `docs/experiments/` and aggregate pages under
`docs/results/` are generated by `scripts/build_wiki.py`. They are publishable
summaries derived from local artifacts. The generated pages are safe to commit;
the ignored source artifacts remain local.
